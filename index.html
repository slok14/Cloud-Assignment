Courses We Offer

We offer the following British degree programs:

BSc (Hons) Computer Systems Engineering (IT)

This program provides a comprehensive understanding of computer systems and IT, preparing students for advanced careers in technology.

BA (Hons) Business and Management (BBA)

This course focuses on developing business acumen and management skills, essential for leadership roles in various industries.

BSc (Hons) International Tourism and Hospitality Management (BHM)

This program equips students with the knowledge and skills required for successful careers in the global tourism and hospitality sector.
